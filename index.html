Our story
begins at sea…



At Salty Anchor, we haul in the day’s freshest catch to grill, smoke, and simmer into dishes that echo the sea. From our Fish of the to Shellfish Add-Ons and Extras, every bite is a tribute to the ocean’s wild heart, served with a salty soul and a fisherman’s grit.

"The ocean’s salt seasons our catch, and the anchor keeps it ours. Grilled, briny, smoked, and bold."

- SALTY ANCHOR
